Frequently Asked Questions

The questions investors ask first — fit, cheque, time, vehicle.

What kind of companies will I be investing in?

To start, mostly outcome-as-a-service and foundational companies — the kind we’re co-building now, like Photon Legal and Curious Men Films — plus fractional-CXO companies, wherever the market is clear and big enough to win. Once we have a few wins behind us, we’ll take bigger, higher-risk bets — think space tech and hardware.

What’s the cheque size?

We’re raising from Indian-origin investors who think in years, not months, and want to be hands-on as we build. Cheques run roughly $25K–$250K, via a single SAFE — indirect exposure to every company we co-build in the next few decades to come.

What’s the time commitment, really?

About ~1 hour a month. The intent is simple: the investors should be genuinely involved with the companies we co-build, and not just be passive investors.

What kind of help are you looking for from investors?

The companies we co-build are selling to the B2B segment, and typically have $100K–$1M ACV.

The help we look for falls into three buckets:

  1. Warm intros — to potential customers and ICPs for these companies.
  2. Expertise on demand — when a company hits something in engineering, product, or GTM, investors with that depth step in.
  3. Market access — our investors span geographies; when a company enters a new market, the investor native to it helps open the door.

There might be diverse set of help needed by our PortCo companies, and we’d be looking up to our investors for that.

Are you a venture capital fund?

No — we’re a venture studio, not a fund. You come in directly on the cap table through a single SAFE: no fund, no AUM, no GP/LP, no management fee. You own a piece of what we build, not units in a vehicle.

What kind of investors are you looking for?

Indian-origin investors who love startups and think in years, not months — founders, operators, angels, and family offices who want to be involved in helping us co-build 100 companies.

How do companies get matched to my expertise?

Once you’re in, we onboard you to Venture OS and map your expertise. As the companies raise specific asks, Venture OS routes the relevant ones to you — so you only ever see where you can genuinely help.
